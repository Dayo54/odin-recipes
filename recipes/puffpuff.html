Description

A very popular West African street food that’s quick and easy to make with different variations. Dangerously delicious and addicting

Ingridents

  • flour
  • yeast
  • warm water
  • vegetable oil
  • sugar
  • salt

steps

  1. Activate the yeast by mixing salt, water, sugar, and yeast then set it aside for 5 minutes
  2. make the dough by adding flour
  3. cover the mix and Let the mixture rise for approximately 1-2 hour
  4. In a large saucepan, pour vegetable oil into a pot until it’s about 3 inches (or 5 centimeters ) high (too little will result into flatter balls), and place in low heat
  5. Make sure the oil is hot enough by putting a drop of batter into the oil. If the oil is not hot enough, the batter will stay at the bottom instead of rising to the top.
  6. scoop the mix bit by bit until it is golden brown
